# Safety Inspection Skill

## Overview

Generate safety inspection reports and hazard identification documents for construction sites.

## Triggers

- "安全检查报告"
- "safety inspection"
- "document:safety"
- "hazard identification"

## Workflow

1. Identify inspection scope and location
2. List applicable safety standards (JGJ 59, JGJ 46, etc.)
3. Generate inspection checklist
4. Record findings with risk ratings
5. Propose corrective measures
6. Output safety disclosure document

## Standards Reference

- JGJ 59-2011 建筑施工安全检查标准
- JGJ 46-2005 施工现场临时用电安全技术规范
- JGJ 80-2016 建筑施工高处作业安全技术规范
- JGJ 130-2011 建筑施工扣件式钢管脚手架安全技术规范

## Output Templates

- 安全技术交底记录
- 安全隐患整改通知单
- 特种作业人员核查表
- 安全防护用品验收记录
