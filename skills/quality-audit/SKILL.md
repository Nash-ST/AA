# Quality Audit Skill

## Overview

Generate quality inspection records and acceptance documents per Chinese national standards.

## Triggers

- "质量验收"
- "quality audit"
- "document:quality"
- "inspection record"

## Workflow

1. Determine inspection batch and subdivision
2. Load applicable GB standard requirements
3. Generate inspection checklist
4. Record measurement data
5. Calculate pass rate
6. Produce acceptance record

## Applicable Standards

| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| GB 50300-2013 | 建筑工程施工质量验收统一标准 |
| GB 50204-2015 | 混凝土结构工程施工质量验收规范 |
| GB 50205-2020 | 钢结构工程施工质量验收标准 |
| GB 50202-2018 | 建筑地基基础工程施工质量验收标准 |
| GB 50209-2010 | 建筑地面工程施工质量验收规范 |

## Output Documents

- 检验批质量验收记录
- 分项工程验收报告
- 材料进场检验报告
- 隐蔽工程验收记录
- 实测实量数据汇总表
